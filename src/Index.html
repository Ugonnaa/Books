CLOCKDOWN

The ultimate social experience for the socially distant.
We present to you a modern era online multiplayer board game for up to four people! As many of us are stuck at home during the lockdown, we are creating the perfect pastime to show that we can still socialise, whilst staying safe. The game is set in key locations such as Manchester, Birmingham, London, Cardiff, Glasgow, Bristol and York.

Your Goal?

Gather as many points as you can while racing against the clock and your peers. The most valuable asset you have is toilet paper. Make sure you hold onto yours. You can collect them, you can steal from your peers, make sure they don't steal from you. This game combines strategy with chance and we wish you the best of luck! The dice can be your best friend, or your worst enemy...
Who will have the most points when the timer runs out?

CHOOSE YOUR FRIENDS WISELY OR THEY MIGHT BETRAY YOU!!!

This 2.5D style game is brought to life with Unity engine and Photon PUN 2 for network connectivity.
